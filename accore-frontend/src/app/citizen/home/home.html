Angeles Fix-It is the official platform for residents to easily report public maintenance needs. Submit issues directly to city departments, track the progress of repairs, and help keep our community safe and beautiful.

How It Works

Improving our city takes three simple steps.

1. Locate the Hazard

Pinpoint the exact location of the issue on the city map to help maintenance crews find it quickly.

2. Snap and Describe

Take a clear photo and provide a brief description of the required maintenance.

3. Track the Fix

Get automated updates as city departments review, process, and resolve your reported issue.

Official Reporting Categories

Angeles Fix-It currently accepts reports for the following municipal maintenance issues. Please select the correct category when submitting to ensure a swift response from the city.

Pothole

Report deep craters and severe road surface damage that pose a risk to vehicles or pedestrians.

Flooding

Report overflowing streets, blocked waterways, and severely inundated public pathways.

Fallen Tree

Report trees or large branches obstructing public roads, sidewalks, or utility power lines.

Clogged Drain

Report blocked municipal sewers, obstructed street gutters, and non-draining water pooling.

Streetlight Out

Report broken, flickering, or completely unlit public streetlights creating neighborhood safety hazards.

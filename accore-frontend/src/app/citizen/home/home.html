How It Works

Improving our city takes three simple steps.

1. Locate the Hazard

Pinpoint the exact location using our interactive map or type in the street address.

2. Snap and Describe

Upload a clear photo and select the category of the issue. Add a brief description to help our field teams.

3. Track the Fix

Submit your report and receive real-time updates as the Angeles City Public Works team resolves the problem.

Official Reporting Categories

AC-CORE currently accepts reports for the following municipal maintenance issues. Please select the correct category when submitting to ensure a swift response from the city.

Streets & Infrastructure

Potholes, Uneven Sidewalks

Report structural damages to public roadways and pedestrian paths that disrupt traffic or pose tripping hazards in public areas.

Sanitation & Waste

Uncollected Garbage, Illegal Dumping

Notify sanitation teams of missed collections, illegal dumping sites, or debris blocking local waterways. Essential for maintaining public health.

Public Utilities

Broken Streetlights, Leaking Pipes

Report malfunctioning municipal lighting and public utility failures to ensure neighborhood visibility and prevent localized flooding.

Public Spaces

Overgrown Vegetation, Fallen Trees

Help maintain the aesthetic and physical safety of Angeles City parks, plazas, and public rights-of-way.
